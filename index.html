Ståle Grut

Doctoral Research Fellow, Dept. of Media and Communication, University of Oslo

About

I am a PhD candidate in media studies at the University of Oslo, working on the project Photofake. My research focuses on fact-checking, journalism, and visual epistemology. I serve on the editorial team of MedieKultur.

Previously, I worked as a journalist and strategic advisor at NRKbeta, NRK's R&D unit. I hold an MA in New Media and Digital Culture from the University of Amsterdam.
